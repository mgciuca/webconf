General Info

The Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science from Ovidius University, Constantza, in cooperation with the Institute of Mathematics "Simion Stoilow" of the Romanian Academy (IMAR) and the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science of University of Missouri - St. Louis, organizes the fourth edition of

Workshop for Young Researchers in Mathematics

The workshop will take place at the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science (Ovidius University, 124 Mamaia Bd., 900527, Constanta, Romania), from Thursday, May 22, to Friday, May 23, 2014.

Preliminary registration.
